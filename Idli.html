Idli

Idli is an extremely simple and easy morning breakfast recipe prepared with rice flour and a small quantity of semolina. It is basically a quick or no-hassle idli recipe that does not require planning, soaking, grounding, or even fermentation. It is lite, and soft and more importantly takes only end-to-end 10 to 15 minutes to cook and serve for morning breakfast.
15 min
Servings
2
Ingredients
  • 1½ cup rice flour, fine
  • ½ cup rava / semolina / suji, coarse
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup curd
  • 1 cup water
  • ½ tsp eno fruit salt
Instructions
  1. Firstly, in a large bowl take 1½ cup rice flour, ½ cup rava and ½ tsp salt.
  2. Add 1 cup curd and 1 cup water.
  3. Mix well making sure there are no lumps. add water as required to form a smooth batter.
  4. Cover and rest for 10 minutes.
  5. After 10 minutes, the batter has absorbed well..
  6. Now add ½ tsp eno fruit salt and mix gently forming a frothy batter.
  7. Pour the idli batter into greased idli plate and keep in steamer.
  8. Steam for 10 minutes or until idli are cooked well.
  9. Finally, enjoy rice flour idli recipe with spicy Sambhar
